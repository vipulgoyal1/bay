Advice on Choosing a Location

The whole Bay Area is very expensive. Are there areas which are more reasonably priced?

It seems that the trend of fully remote work is now ending. However, many people need to go to the office only two or three times a week. In that case, it makes a lot of sense to consider areas like Gilroy or Morgan Hill. These areas have beautiful big, relatively new houses and are reasonably safe. All for prices much lower than San Jose, or Palo Alto, or San Francisco.

My primary criteria is a beautiful view of the hills. Can you recommend some areas for me?

 Absolutely. I would suggest you look into Castro Valley, Hayward, Morgan Hill, and the Alum Rock area of San Jose.

My primary criteria is a good public school district. However, it seems that all such areas are super expensive. What would you suggest?

I would suggest you look into Los Gatos Hills. It has one of the best school districts in the Bay Area and yet reasonably priced houses. You can easily find houses with a large plot of land with trees all around you. On the flip side, the nearest shopping places can be up to 15 to 20 min drive away.

I want to live in a nice downtown with everything within walking distance. Which areas would you suggest?

I would suggest you look into Mountain View, Palo Alto, San Francisco and Oakland. All these places have pretty well-developed downtowns.
